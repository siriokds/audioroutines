Features:

- Play CH3 4 channels modules ('M.K.' mods converted to CH3)!
- Compressed patterns
- Compressed samples - (RLE and RL-Delta compression)
- Autodetection of samples type for compression
- Module infos (name, samples name) zapped out!
- Included MOD2CHP3 converter



Effects supported:

0xy - Arpeggio
1xy - Porta Up
2xy - Porta Down
3xy - Porta to Note
4xy - Vibrato
5xy - Porta + Volume Slide
6xy - Vibrato + Volume Slide
Axy - Volume Slide
Bxy - Jump to Pattern
Cxy - Set Volume
Dxy - Pattern Break
Exx - Extended Commands:
      E1x - Fine Portamento Up 
      E2x - Fine Portamento Down
      EAx - Fine Volume Slide Up
      EBx - Fine Volume Slide Down
      EEx - Pattern Delay
Fxy - Set Speed (not BPM speed change!)
